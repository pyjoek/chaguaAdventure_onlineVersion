Hotel Riu Jambo Zanzibar

The Hotel Riu Jambo is a 4-star all-inclusive resort located on Nungwi Beach in Zanzibar, Tanzania. It is the newest addition to the RIU Hotels & Resorts portfolio and opened its doors in June 2022. The hotel has a total of 300 rooms and suites, all of which are spacious and well-appointed.

The Hotel Riu Jambo offers a wide range of amenities and activities for guests of all ages. The resort has four swimming pools, including one with a swim-up bar, a children's pool, and a lazy river. There is also a fitness center, a spa, a kids' club, and a teen club.

Location: Nungwi Beach, Zanzibar, Tanzania
Dining options: Buffet restaurant, Italian restaurant, Asian restaurant, swim-up bar, 24-hour snack bar.

Zuri Zanzibar Hotel & Resort

Zuri Zanzibar is a 5-star beach resort located in Kendwa village, on the west-facing shores of Unguja, the main island of Zanzibar. The resort is just 50 km from Stone Town and its international airport. Zuri Zanzibar offers a stunning white-sand beach, turquoise waters, and unforgettable sunsets. The resort has a private 300-meter beach that is free of any tide-impact, allowing for safe swimming all day long.

The resort's bungalows, suites, and villas are fitted with a mix of stylish contemporary furniture and local artisanal features and artwork. All rooms flow seamlessly out to generous terraces surrounded by lush tropical gardens.

Zuri Zanzibar offers a fusion of European, African, Arabic, and Indian cuisine, creating a rainbow of sensational flavors and bringing the very best of Indian Ocean cuisine. Experience unforgettable dining in one of the resort's three restaurants and four bars.

The resort also has a spa, fitness center, and a variety of activities and excursions to choose from, including swimming, sunbathing, snorkeling, diving, sailing, windsurfing, kayaking, and massages.

Zuri Zanzibar is the perfect place to relax and unwind in a beautiful and luxurious setting. The resort offers something for everyone, from couples to families to solo travelers.

Gold Zanzibar Beach House & Spa

Gold Zanzibar is a luxury beach resort located in Kendwa, Zanzibar, Tanzania. The resort is set on a secluded stretch of white-sand beach and offers stunning views of the Indian Ocean. Gold Zanzibar features rooms, suites, and villas, all of which are spacious and luxuriously appointed. The resort also has a variety of amenities, including a swimming pool, a spa, a fitness center, a dive center, and several restaurants and bars.

Gold Zanzibar is the perfect place to relax and unwind. The resort offers a variety of activities and amenities to keep guests entertained, including swimming, sunbathing, diving, snorkeling, and fishing. Gold Zanzibar also has a number of spa treatments available, perfect for a relaxing day of pampering.

In addition to its luxurious accommodations and amenities, Gold Zanzibar is also committed to sustainability. The resort uses solar power to generate electricity and has a number of water conservation initiatives in place. Gold Zanzibar is also a member of the Green Globe 21 sustainable tourism program.
Gold Zanzibar is the perfect place to experience the beauty and luxury of Zanzibar.

Neptune Pwani Beach Resort & Spa

Neptune Pwani Beach Resort & Spa is a 5-star all-inclusive resort located in Kiwengwa, Zanzibar, Tanzania. The resort is situated on the north-east coast of Zanzibar, on the Kiwengwa shoreline, and approximately 45 km from the International Airport, next to Stone Town.

The resort features rooms all with a terrace and ocean view, situated in 2-level blocks and 4 individual beachfront suites with an in-room Jacuzzi. All air-conditioned rooms boast traditional Zanzibar décor and have a furnished balcony, a private bathroom with a shower, and a flat-screen TV. Free Wi-Fi is provided in all rooms and public areas.

The resort has 6 restaurants offering local cuisine, seafood, Indian, and Thai cuisine. There are also 3 bars. The Earth and Rain Spa features a sauna, steam bath, pool, and offers massage and beauty treatments.

The resort offers a variety of activities and amenities, including:

  • 2 freshwater pools
  • Tropical garden
  • Sauna, steam bath, massage, and beauty treatments
  • Diving, snorkeling, windsurfing, kitesurfing, catamaran sailing
  • Stand-up paddleboarding, fishing, tennis, volleyball
  • Kids club, playground, and table games
  • 24-hour front desk, car rental, airport shuttle
The resort is a popular destination for couples, families, and groups. It is a great place to relax and enjoy the beautiful beaches of Zanzibar.

TUI BLUE Bahari Zanzibar

TUI BLUE Bahari Zanzibar is a 5-star beachfront resort styled like an Arabian palace, located in Kiwengwa, Zanzibar, Tanzania. The resort is set on a white-sand beach and features two swimming pools, a spa, a fitness center, and a variety of restaurants and bars. Guests can also enjoy numerous activities, including water sports, tennis, and a kids' club.

The resort is a popular choice for couples and families alike. It offers various room types, including standard rooms, suites, and villas. All rooms are spacious and comfortable, with many featuring private balconies or terraces with ocean views.

The resort’s restaurants serve a mix of international and local cuisines. Several bars on-site provide a relaxed atmosphere for enjoying drinks and snacks. The spa offers a variety of treatments, such as massages, facials, and body wraps, while the well-equipped fitness center features state-of-the-art machines.

TUI BLUE Bahari Zanzibar is the perfect destination to relax and experience the beauty of Zanzibar. With its extensive amenities and friendly staff, guests are guaranteed a luxurious and memorable stay.

Baraza Resort and Spa

Baraza Resort and Spa Zanzibar is a luxury all-inclusive resort located on Bwejuu Beach in Zanzibar, Tanzania. The resort features 30 spacious villas, each with a private plunge pool and terrace. Designed in traditional Swahili style, the villas offer breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean.

Guests can enjoy top-notch amenities, including two swimming pools, a spa, a fitness center, a kids' club, and several fine dining restaurants and bars. A wide range of activities and excursions are available, such as snorkeling, diving, sailing, and kayaking.

Baraza Resort and Spa is the perfect destination to unwind and immerse yourself in the beauty of Zanzibar. With a combination of luxury, relaxation, and adventure, the resort ensures a memorable stay with excellent service and hospitality.

Park Hyatt Zanzibar

Park Hyatt Zanzibar is one of the most luxurious properties in Stone Town. Located on the seafront, just a short walk from the town center, it offers a peaceful retreat from the bustling alleyways.

The hotel's elegantly designed rooms and suites feature modern amenities, including air conditioning, minibars, and flat-screen TVs. Many rooms also come with private balconies or terraces offering breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean.

Guests can unwind at the hotel's spa, which offers a range of treatments, including massages, facials, and body wraps. The property also features a well-equipped fitness center, a swimming pool, a kids' club, and diverse dining options serving both international and local cuisine.

Park Hyatt Zanzibar is also ideal for business travelers, offering professional business services such as laptop rentals. The hotel maintains the highest standards of an international luxury property.

Emerald Zanzibar Resort & Spa

Emerald Zanzibar is a 5-star resort located in Matemwe, Zanzibar, Tanzania. Opened in 2019 and owned by Emerald Resorts, this luxurious retreat offers a serene escape blending the exotic natural environment with modern elegance.

Emerald Zanzibar is a popular destination for both business and leisure travelers. The resort is conveniently located near Matemwe's attractions, including the Mnemba Atoll, the Mnemba Marine Reserve, and the Matemwe Spice Farm. The resort offers various amenities and services designed to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable stay.

The luxury suites combine African style with modern comforts. The Emerald Spa offers world-class treatments from Thai and Balinese cultures, making it the perfect place for relaxation and rejuvenation.

With its spacious and comfortable rooms, friendly staff, and beautiful location, Emerald Zanzibar is an excellent choice for those seeking a luxurious and relaxing stay in Matemwe.

Tulia Zanzibar Beach Resort

Tulia Zanzibar is a luxury boutique hotel located on the east coast of Zanzibar, Tanzania, offering breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean.

The hotel features 16 spacious villas, each with a private terrace or balcony overlooking the ocean. The villas are elegantly decorated in a modern style and come with air conditioning, Wi-Fi, and flat-screen TVs.

Guests can enjoy a variety of amenities, including a swimming pool, spa, fitness center, and a selection of restaurants and bars. For those seeking adventure, the hotel offers activities like snorkeling, diving, and sailing.

In response to the lack of beach space at high tide, Tulia has created a small artificial beach below the pool area, with loungers and parasols. However, this space may not accommodate all guests when the hotel is full.

White Paradise Zanzibar

White Paradise Zanzibar is a boutique resort located on the east coast of Zanzibar, Tanzania, set on a private beach with stunning views of the Indian Ocean. It is the perfect place to relax and unwind, offering beautiful gardens, an infinity pool, and delicious food.

Conveniently located in the Pongwe area, this 4-star property provides easy access to local attractions and dining options. White Paradise Zanzibar offers a variety of accommodation options, from standard rooms to villas with private pools.

The resort features a wide range of amenities, including yoga classes, massages, and boat trips. The friendly and welcoming staff is always ready to help guests make the most of their stay.

Whether you're visiting as a couple, with family, or with friends, White Paradise Zanzibar offers something for everyone – from stunning views and delicious food to a variety of activities. It's the ideal place to relax and enjoy a peaceful vacation.

Zanzibar Serena Hotel

Zanzibar Serena Hotel is a 5-star hotel located in Stone Town, Zanzibar, Tanzania. Opened in 1997, it is owned and operated by Serena Hotels, a renowned Tanzanian hotel chain.

The hotel offers 5-star accommodation with stunning views of the Indian Ocean, an outdoor pool with sun loungers, a café, and two restaurants. Zanzibar Serena Hotel also provides free private parking.

All rooms are beautifully decorated with traditional furnishings and include amenities such as satellite TV, a minibar, a tea and coffee maker, and a private bathroom with a bath, shower, and free toiletries. The hotel also offers 24-hour room service and free Wi-Fi, as well as a fitness center and a spa.

Zanzibar Serena Hotel is a popular choice for both business and leisure travelers. Its prime location in Stone Town allows easy access to many attractions, including the House of Wonders, the Sultan's Palace, and the Old Fort. The hotel offers a wide range of services and amenities to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable stay for all guests.

Z Hotel Zanzibar

Z Hotel is the original boutique hotel on Zanzibar, opening in 2010. It brings casual relaxation blended with contemporary London-inspired design to the beaches of Zanzibar, offering the 21st-century traveler a fresh and original beach holiday experience.

The Z Hotel is a contemporary property with a relaxed atmosphere. It offers rooms and suites, all decorated in a modern style, and features an infinity pool, a spa, and a restaurant.

Located close to many of Nungwi's attractions, including Nungwi Beach, the Nungwi Lighthouse, and the Nungwi Market, the hotel is popular with both business and leisure travelers. The hotel also offers a variety of amenities and services designed to ensure that guests have a comfortable and enjoyable stay.

The Z Hotel is a great choice for travelers looking for a stylish and relaxing stay in Nungwi. The hotel provides a range of amenities and services to make every guest's stay as comfortable and enjoyable as possible.
